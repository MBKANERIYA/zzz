Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between bookkeeping and accounting?

Bookkeeping is the day-to-day recording of financial transactions — data entry, bank reconciliation, invoicing. Accounting goes further: preparing formal financial statements, analysing performance, managing compliance, and providing strategic financial guidance. We offer both, and they work best together.

Do you offer online accounting?

Yes. We use cloud accounting platforms (Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks) that allow you to access your financials from anywhere. All communication, document sharing, and reporting can be handled remotely if that's your preference.

Can I outsource my accounting instead of hiring someone?

Absolutely. Many of our clients outsource their entire accounting function to us. It's typically more cost-effective than a part-time or full-time hire, and you benefit from qualified oversight and consistent quality.

How often will I receive financial reports?

We offer monthly, quarterly, or annual reporting depending on your needs. Most growing businesses find monthly reporting gives them the best visibility and control.

Are your services suitable for someone just starting a business?

Yes. We work with startups regularly — from initial ABN and company setup through to ongoing accounting and tax lodgement. Getting your accounting right from day one saves significant time and money later.
