Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you update my books?

We offer both weekly and monthly bookkeeping cycles. The right frequency depends on your transaction volume and reporting needs. Most small businesses find monthly updates sufficient, while retail and hospitality businesses often prefer weekly.

Do you work with my existing accounting software?

Yes. We work primarily with Xero and MYOB, and can also support QuickBooks and other cloud platforms. If you're not currently using any software, we'll recommend the best option for your business and handle the setup.

Can I outsource bookkeeping instead of hiring someone in-house?

Absolutely — and many of our clients do exactly that. Outsourcing your bookkeeping to us is often more cost-effective than a part-time hire, and you get the benefit of qualified oversight rather than relying on a single employee.

Will my books be ready for BAS and tax time?

Yes. We maintain your records to be BAS-ready and tax-ready at all times. When your BAS or annual return is due, there's no last-minute catch-up required — we simply hand the data across to your tax accountant (or prepare it ourselves if you use our BAS and tax services too).

What if my books are a mess right now?

That's fine — we deal with this regularly. We'll start with a data clean-up to bring your records up to date, reconcile any discrepancies, and then transition into an ongoing bookkeeping arrangement.

How do I share documents with you?

You can share documents through your cloud accounting platform, our secure file upload portal, or email. We'll set up the simplest workflow for your situation during onboarding.

Do you only work with Sydney-based businesses?

We're based in Sydney (Harris Park area) and work with many local businesses across Sydney's western suburbs. However, because we use cloud accounting platforms, we also serve clients right across Australia.
