Frequently Asked Questions

How often can you process payroll?

We offer weekly, fortnightly, and monthly payroll processing — whichever matches your business's pay cycle. Most of our clients run fortnightly or monthly.

Do you handle superannuation payments?

We calculate super guarantee amounts for each employee and ensure the figures are correct. The actual payment to super funds is typically made by the employer through a clearing house or super platform — but we'll guide you through the process and flag deadlines.

What is Single Touch Payroll and do I need it?

STP is an ATO requirement for all employers in Australia. It means payroll information — including salaries, tax withheld, and super — is reported to the ATO each time you run payroll. We handle STP lodgement as part of our standard service.

Can you take over payroll from my current provider?

Yes. We regularly transition clients from other providers or from self-managed payroll. We'll review your existing data, ensure everything is correct, and take over processing from the next pay cycle.

What happens if I make a late super payment?

Late super payments can attract the Super Guarantee Charge (SGC), which includes interest, an administration fee, and a potential penalty. We help you stay ahead of super deadlines to avoid this.

Is outsourcing payroll more cost-effective than doing it in-house?

For most small to medium businesses, yes. Outsourcing eliminates the need for a dedicated payroll staff member, reduces the risk of errors, and ensures you stay compliant — all at a predictable monthly cost.
