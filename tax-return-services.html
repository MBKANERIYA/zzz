Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my tax refund?

Once your return is lodged, the ATO generally processes refunds within 2 to 14 business days. If your return is flagged for review, it may take longer — but we'll keep you informed throughout.

Can you lodge my tax return if I've missed the deadline?

Yes. We regularly help clients with late or overdue returns, including those from previous financial years. We'll work through each year and minimise any penalties where possible.

What deductions can I claim?

It depends on your occupation and income type. Common deductions include work-related travel, home office expenses, tools and equipment, uniforms, self-education, and vehicle costs. We'll review your specific situation and ensure nothing is missed.

Do I need to visit your office?

No. We offer a fully online service — you can upload documents, communicate with your accountant, and receive your completed return without leaving home. In-person appointments are also available if you prefer.

Is it worth using a tax agent instead of myTax?

For simple returns, myTax can work. But if you have multiple income sources, deductions beyond the basics, investment income, or an ABN — a qualified tax agent will almost certainly save you more than the fee costs. We also extend your lodgement deadline when we manage your return.

What documents do I need to provide?

At minimum, you'll need your income statement (available through myGov), details of any other income (rental, investment, ABN), and records of work-related expenses. We'll send you a checklist based on your situation after our initial consultation.

Where is your office located?

We're based in Sydney, in the Harris Park area. We serve clients across Sydney and Australia-wide through our secure online service. You can visit us in person or work with us entirely online — whichever suits you.
